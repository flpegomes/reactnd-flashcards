The best flashcard app for learning Korean.

Report an issue

Pre-installed decks and cards.

There are nearly 6,000 pre-installed flashcards from the Korean dictionary to get you started.

Create your own.

Add your own flashcards or flashcard decks. Edit and delete as you see fit. Add or remove cards from your decks.

Study Sessions and Quizzes

Choose whether or not to do a study session (score not recorded), or a quiz (stats recorded and saved).

Stay Disciplined.

Track your progress with the calendar at the top, set goals, get reminders, and practice everyday.

Personalized.

As you mark words "New", "Learning", or "Mastered", they are distributed into different sets with different study frequencies.

(Forthcoming feature: Study frequencies are based on the Leitner Spaced Repetition Model.)
